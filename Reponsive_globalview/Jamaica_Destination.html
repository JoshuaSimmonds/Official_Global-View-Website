Location and Geography


Jamaica, a jewel island located in the Caribbean sea, is globally appreciated and celebrated for its vibrant culture, stunning beaches, rivers and rhythmic music.


Culture


Worldwide famous for being the birthplace of reggae music and home to legendary figures like Bob Marley and world record holding athletes such as Usain Bolt, Shelly Ann Fraser-Pryce.


Airports


Sangster International Airport (MBJ) in Montego Bay and Norman Manley International Airport (KIN) in Kingston.


Climate


Tropical climate ranging from 25°C to 31°C.


Currency


The Jamaican Dollar (JMD), While many establishments accept US dollars
